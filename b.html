SAS Multicultural Quest Beppu Trails

Baasankhuu Sainzaya 13423014

Although we have faced some challenges to organizing this event and even discussed about cancelling it, we have still successfully completed it.

Promoting the event

Initially, there wasn't many participants who registered, and we almost came to the point to cancel the event due to the low number of participants. Then, the day before the registration was over, we all went to cafeteria and promoted our event together. This was a success and the number of participants increased until the number that we agreed to organize if reached.

Challenges and Activities

The challenges started all the way starting when we were brainstorming ideas. We couldn't generate interesting activity ideas and spent a long time thinking. After coming up with ideas, we have decided to have a walk around Beppu's famous places including Kannawa, Beppu Eki, and Beppu Tower. At the event day, there was another challenge. It was raining hard! We discussed whether to cancerl the event or not and ultimately, decided to organize despite the heavy rainfall.

Seeing Beppu from top

Seeing Beppu from top was a one-of-a-kind experience. The sea was so beautiful and I could see all the houses and cars. At the top floor, there was cute plushees and other souvenir items. At the floor under it, there was a magical room that makes you feel like you're in the galaxy with help of lighting.
