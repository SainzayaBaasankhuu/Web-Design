# SAS Website Project

This project is a simple HTML/CSS website created to introduce my experience as a member of **SAS (Student Activity Station)** at Ritsumeikan Asia Pacific University (APU).  
It showcases two major cultural exchange events I helped organize: **Multicultural Quest** and **Beppu Trails**.

## About SAS

**SAS (Student Activity Station)** is a student‑led organization at APU that plans and hosts **local cultural exchange events**.  
Its mission is to:

- Connect APU students with the Beppu community  
- Promote cultural understanding and diversity  
- Provide hands‑on experience in event planning, teamwork, and communication  
- Create opportunities for students to learn about Japanese culture and share their own  

As a member of SAS, I participated in event design, logistics, promotion, and on‑site facilitation.
