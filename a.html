SAS Multicultural Quest Beppu Trails

Baasankhuu Sainzaya 13423014

For this event, we were tasked to come up with new ideas for the first-ever Multicultural Quest. It was decided that the theme is Oita so we have decided to have ice-breaking games and bamboo-creating session.

Ice-breaking game

Oita is an amazing city renowned for its Onsen and bamboo production. As a person who enjoys travelling to Oita city, it was fun for me to generate activity ideas that'll help participants feel closer to Oita. I initially proposed as part of the activities to let the participants create presentation and present to each other, but it was not included in the final event. For the final event, we had ice-breaking game, where we have asked participants to find commonalities among them, next, to complete the crossword game which has all Oita-related things.

Bamboo crafting

For the main part of the event, we invited Bamboo bell craft teachers and let the participants create bamboo bell for themselves. It seemed a little hard since it's very detailed but at the end, everyone succeeded.

My experience at Oita

Oita is the first city I went to since I came to Japan. My impression was that it's a peaceful, beatiful city and much larger and developed than Beppu. Since it's easy and fast to go to Oita, I go to Oita once in a while to hang out with my friends.
